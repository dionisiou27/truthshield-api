The Visibility Gap

Verification capacity and algorithmic reach are decoupled. Defenders verify rigorously and reach no one; attackers verify nothing and reach everyone. The high-verification / high-reach quadrant is empty — TruthShield is built to occupy it without becoming the thing it counters.

Why concentrate — the Lighthouse evidence

0.1 % of users → 80 % of fake-news exposures (Grinberg 2019)
Concentration means blanket response is waste. The system ranks claims by visibility and amplification, not by volume of falsehood, and intervenes where one answer shifts an entire thread.
~100 accounts → ~70 % of conspiracy content (MEO 2026)
National-scale confirmation. A lighthouse does not light the whole ocean — it stands where the hazard is.
Temporal assessment is Kairos, made technical
STABLE claims (settled science) allow direct correction. LIVE_REQUIRED claims (war, elections) demand Tier-A fresh sourcing and hedged, timestamped language before any candidate is generated. AMBIGUOUS + weak evidence routes to CAUTIOUS mode.
