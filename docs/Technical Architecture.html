00Why this exists/

The Visibility Gap

Verification capacity and algorithmic reach are decoupled. Defenders verify rigorously and reach no one; attackers verify nothing and reach everyone. The high-verification / high-reach quadrant is empty — TruthShield is built to occupy it without becoming the thing it counters.

Why concentrate — the Lighthouse evidence

0.1 % of users → 80 % of fake-news exposures (Grinberg 2019)
Concentration means blanket response is waste. The system ranks claims by visibility and amplification, not by volume of falsehood, and intervenes where one answer shifts an entire thread.

03The cross-border layer/

The Mesh — simulate a wave

Coordinated campaigns cross borders in hours; institutional responses take days. Nodes share pattern metadata — never content, never directives. The mesh proposes; sovereignty disposes.

What the mesh is not
Not a federation (no central directives) · not a content-sharing network (reference and timing, no republication) · not a fact-check pool (verification stays local) · not a substitute for political coordination · not a defence alliance (civilian-led, EU-compliance-first).
One role, many faces
Guardian is a function, not an account. Each node runs its own instance — same role definition, own language, own sources, own cultural register. The mesh shares the architecture of the role; the faces stay national. Nothing crosses a border except pattern metadata.
04Why this never becomes what it counters/

Immutable Constraints & Red Lines

LEARNABLE — THE HOW

  • tone variant (4 buckets)
  • source-mix strategy
  • length within limits
  • delivery timing

IMMUTABLE — THE WHAT

  • factual content & evidence
  • source authority weights
  • claim classification logic
  • boundary definitions · IO thresholds

Red lines, node and mesh level: no election interference · no micro-targeting of individuals · no covert operations · no exploitation of vulnerabilities · no undisclosed automation · no licensing to non-democratic actors. Violation triggers immediate de-meshing. The axis of distinction is automated versus organic — never opinion. The body removes nothing. It responds.

Lies do not defeat truth. Silence does.

TruthShield carries only until it is no longer needed — until human voices are louder than the artificial ones again.
