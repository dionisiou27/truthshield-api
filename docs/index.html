How TruthShield Works

Our ML pipeline processes claims through multiple analysis stages for comprehensive assessment.

1

Detect incoming claims

Text, links, or emerging narratives are ingested in real time via API or monitoring streams.

2

Analyze risk and coordination

Claims are assessed for credibility, virality, and coordinated manipulation patterns.

3

Verify against trusted sources

Information is cross-checked against curated, transparent, and auditable evidence sources.

4

Respond with evidence

A clear verdict and an evidence-based counter-narrative are generated for visible intervention.
